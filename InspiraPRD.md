Product Requirements Document (PRD): Design Inspiration & Professional Network
1. Project Overview
The platform is a specialized visual network focused exclusively on design disciplines, including graphic, multimedia, industrial, and fashion design, as well as architecture. Unlike general inspiration tools, it emphasizes the direct link between a project and its creator to ensure professional visibility and ethical content usage.
+3

2. Target Audience
The platform is designed for users aged 18–40, primarily within the creative ecosystem:


Designers: Emerging professionals and freelancers seeking to showcase original work, build profiles, and monetize their skills.
+1


Design-Oriented Users: Students and professionals looking for high-quality, academic-focused references and potential collaborators.
+1

3. Core Features & Functional Specifications
3.1 High Priority (Must Have)

User Registration & Profiles: Separate account types for designers and general users.
+1


Project Management: Designers can upload images, videos, and descriptions for original projects with mandatory authorship attribution.
+2


Curated Inspiration Boards: Tools for users to save and organize projects by discipline, technique, or theme.
+1


Search & Discovery: Advanced filtering by design field and creative approach to ensure relevance.
+3

3.2 Medium Priority (Should Have)

Direct Messaging: In-platform private communication between users and designers to facilitate collaboration.
+1


Marketplace: Features allowing designers to list and sell original products or services directly to users.
+1


Moderation Tools: Basic reporting and administrative tools to maintain professional standards.
+1

4. User Experience & Interface (The "Surface")
The platform utilizes a minimalist, "artsy," and editorial aesthetic to reflect high design standards.


Visual Style: A sophisticated atmosphere using dark/neutral backgrounds to make the creative work the focal point.


Typography: A professional hierarchy combining elegant display fonts for titles with clean, legible typefaces for body text.


Skeleton Structure: An intuitive layout featuring a persistent sidebar for navigation and a prominent search bar for efficient exploration.
+1

5. Success Metrics

Engagement: Frequency of content uploads and volume of interactions (likes, saves, comments).


Growth: Total number of active registered users and growth in designer followers.
+1


Monetization: Volume and value of transactions through the integrated marketplace.

6. Out of Scope (Initial Release)
Live video streaming or real-time workshops.

AI-generated design recommendations.

Social media-style "stories" or short-form video feeds.

External job boards.
